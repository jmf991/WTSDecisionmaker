Welcome

This platform offers you support in tax decision-making for your individual business transaction. The completed decision-making is documented in accordance with the legal requirements. All decisions offered on this plattform were created, tested and checked by tax advisors from the central tax department (FF) based on the current tax laws. Therefore, the result of the decision-making process is the answer to your tax queries in your business transaction. Further involvement of the responsible persons in the tax department is not necessary. If you have any questions about your issue, you will be contacted by the tax department. Please feel free to give us your open feedback on this platform at any time. Sincerely, Your Tax Department
